### Ingredients:
- 1 cup oats
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup flour
- 2 tbsp chocolate powder
- A pinch of salt

### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the wet ingredients:**
   - In a medium saucepan, melt the butter, peanut butter, and milk together over medium heat, stirring constantly until smooth.

2. **Combine the dry ingredients:**
   - In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, sugar, chocolate powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.

3. **Mix wet and dry ingredients:**
   - Pour the melted butter and peanut butter mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined.

4. **Set the mixture:**
   - Line a baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease it with butter. Pour the mixture into the dish and press it down evenly.

5. **Chill:**
   - Place the dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to set. This will give the bars a firm texture.

6. **Serve:**
   - Once set, cut into bars and serve. Enjoy your delicious Chocolate Peanut Butter Oat Bars!

These bars are a perfect blend of chocolate and peanut butter, with a chewy texture from the oats. Enjoy!
