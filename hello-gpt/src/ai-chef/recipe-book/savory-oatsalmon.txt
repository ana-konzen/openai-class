### Savory Oat-Crusted Salmon

Here is a delicious savory dish you can make for lunch using ingredients from your pantry.

#### Ingredients:
- 2 salmon fillets
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons parmesan, grated
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Oat Crust:**
   - In a bowl, combine the oats, grated parmesan, salt, and pepper.
   
2. **Prepare the Egg Wash:**
   - In another bowl, beat the egg.

3. **Coat the Salmon:**
   - Dip each salmon fillet into the beaten egg, then press it into the oat mixture, ensuring the fillet is well-coated.

4. **Cook the Salmon:**
   - In a large skillet, heat the butter and sesame oil over medium heat.
   - Once the butter is melted and the pan is hot, add the oat-crusted salmon fillets.
   - Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the crust is golden brown and the salmon is cooked to your desired level of doneness.

5. **Serve:**
   - Serve the oat-crusted salmon fillets hot, perhaps with a side of cottage cheese or a simple salad if you have any greens available.

Enjoy your savory oat-crusted salmon for lunch!
