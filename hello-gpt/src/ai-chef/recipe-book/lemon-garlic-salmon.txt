### Savory Dinner Recipe: Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

#### Ingredients:
- Salmon fillets
- Butter
- Lemon (sliced)
- Garlic (minced)
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Paprika
- Broccoli
- Carrots
- Bell peppers
- Potatoes (optional)
- Fresh parsley (optional, if you have any)

#### Instructions:

1. **Preheat the Oven:**
   Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. **Prepare the Vegetables:**
   - Wash and chop the broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers into bite-sized pieces. If you want to add potatoes, chop them into small cubes.
   - Place the chopped vegetables in a large bowl.
   - Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt, black pepper, and paprika. Toss to coat evenly.

3. **Roast the Vegetables:**
   - Spread the seasoned vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer.
   - Roast in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly crispy at the edges.

4. **Prepare the Lemon Garlic Butter:**
   - In a small saucepan, melt about 2-3 tablespoons of butter over medium heat.
   - Add minced garlic (about 2-3 cloves) and cook for 1-2 minutes until it's fragrant.
   - Add a squeeze of lemon juice (reserving the slices for later) and stir to combine. Remove from heat.

5. **Prepare the Salmon:**
   - Place the salmon fillets on another baking sheet lined with parchment paper or lightly greased with olive oil.
   - Brush the fillets generously with the lemon garlic butter.
   - Season with salt and black pepper.
   - Place lemon slices on top of each fillet for extra flavor.

6. **Bake the Salmon:**
   - Bake in the oven (you can place the salmon in the oven along with the vegetables for the last 12-15 minutes of roasting) until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork, about 12-15 minutes.

7. **Serve:**
   - Transfer the roasted vegetables to a serving platter.
   - Place the baked salmon fillets on top.
   - Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

8. **Enjoy:**
   - Serve your Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon with Roasted Vegetables hot and enjoy a delicious, savory dinner.

### Sweet Dessert Option: Chocolate Peanut Butter Oat Bars

#### Ingredients:
- Oats
- Peanut butter
- Chocolate powder
- Butter
- Honey
- Vanilla extract

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Base:**
   - In a microwave-safe bowl, combine 1/2 cup of peanut butter and 1/4 cup of butter. Microwave until melted and smooth, about 1 minute.
   - Add 1/3 cup of honey and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the mixture. Stir to combine.

2. **Add the Oats:**
   - Add 2 cups of oats to the mixture and stir until the oats are fully coated.

3. **Prepare the Chocolate Layer:**
   - In another microwave-safe bowl, combine 1/2 cup of chocolate powder and 1/4 cup of peanut butter. Microwave until melted, about 1 minute, and stir until smooth.

4. **Assemble the Bars:**
   - Press the oat mixture into the bottom of a lined or greased baking dish to form an even layer.
   - Pour the chocolate mixture over the oat base and spread it evenly.

5. **Chill:**
   - Place the dish in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until the bars are firm and set.

6. **Serve:**
   - Cut into bars and enjoy your sweet treat after dinner.

This combination gives you a savory, nutritious dinner with a sweet and satisfying dessert, all using ingredients from your pantry.
